Chef Beazy's Chicken Stew

Description

Sweet, soft chicken that just slides off the bone!!

This recipe has been in my family for over 40 years, and has been passed down for 3 generations. It is very easy to learn, but it can take years to be able to master it. Have fun trying different variations of your own, the world definitely needs more good food.

Ingredients

  • 1 package of chicken legs or thighs
  • 3 tbsp oil (any type will do)
  • 3 tsp of sugar (brown sugar preferred)
  • 2 potatoes
  • 2 carrots (optional)
  • 1 onion
  • 4 cups of water
  • 1 clove of garlic (chopped)
  • 1 tsp of salt
  • 1 tsp of black pepper
  • a pinch of curry powder
  • 1 tbsp of Adobo (optional)
  • 1 tbsp of Bahia Complete Seasoning (optional)

Directions

  1. Start off by chopping up all the vegetables
  2. Grab a soup-size pot, and turn the stove on medium heat
  3. Add the oil to your pot
  4. Next add about the sugar to the oil
  5. Stir the sugar around until it starts to brown
  6. The longer you leave the sugar, the darker the stew
  7. Next add the chicken and stir fry until itâ€™s firm on the outside
  8. You should see the chicken start turning brown and smooth
  9. Add water to the pot (enough to completely cover the chicken)
  10. Now bring your soup mixture to a boil (high heat)
  11. Add the veggies to pot and let the soup boil for about 15 minutes
  12. Turn stove back down to medium heat and add your seasonings
  13. Let pot simmer for about 30 minutes before the first taste test
  14. Adjust your seasonings accordingly
  15. Boil the stew until you get your desired consistency
